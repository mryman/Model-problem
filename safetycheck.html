This is a climber safety check prep list

Please answer questions below. These steps are representative of what we expect to see when you complete a safety check in person with one of our staff members prior to being admitted to our facility

Eligibility Section

Do you have a climbing harness?

Do you have a climbing rope?

Do you have a belay device?

Do you have a locking carabiner?


Physical / Visual Inspection Steps

Do you know how to inspect your harness for correct sizing, fit, and orientation?

Do you know how to inspect your partner's harness for these factors as well?

Can you verify when your harness buckle is doubled back?

Can you verify when your partner's harness buckle is doubled back?

Can you demonstrate how to tie a rewoven figure eight knot?

Can you demonstrate how to properly load a belay device?

Do you know how to confirm the carabiner on the device is locked?


Verbal Communication Steps

What two words should a climber say to verify his/her belayer is ready?

What two word reply from a belayer indicates he/she is ready?

What should a climber say to his/her belayer just before starting up a climb?

What two words should a belayer say to affirm a climber may begin?
